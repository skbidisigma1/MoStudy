
# 2025–2026 Competitive Events Guidelines  
## International Business (High School)

**Updated:** November 2025  
**Pages:** 1–11

---

## Event Description

International Business gives members the opportunity to explore the dynamic global economy and understand how businesses operate across borders. Through an objective test and a role play scenario, members apply their knowledge of international trade, cultural awareness, global marketing, and economic systems.

---

## Event Overview

- **Division:** High School  
- **Event Type:** Team of 1, 2, or 3 members  
- **Event Category:** Role Play  
- **Event Elements:** Objective Test and Interactive Role Play

## Event Administration

### Objective Test

- 100 multiple-choice questions  
- **Time:** 50 minutes  
- Online, proctored, on-site  
- No reference materials  
- Devices must be powered off  
- Online calculator provided  
- Team scores are averaged  

### Role Play Advancement

Top **15 teams** advance based on team-averaged test score.

---

## Interactive Role Play

### Timing
- Preparation: 20 minutes (1-minute warning)  
- Presentation: 7 minutes (1-minute warning)  
- Q&A: None  

### Rules
- One shared prompt per team  
- Two notecards per competitor (both sides allowed)  
- No technology, visuals, or props  
- Judges may ask questions  
- Closed audience  
- Prompt confidentiality required  

---

## Scoring

- All decisions final

## Role Play Presentation Rating Sheet (100 Points)

### 1. Understanding of Role Play & Problem Definition (0–10)
- No description or problems defined (0)
- Synopsis OR problems defined (1–6)
- Synopsis AND problems defined (7–8)
- Expert understanding (9–10)

### 2. Alternatives & Analysis (0–20)
- No alternatives (0)
- Alternatives without analysis (1–9)
- Two alternatives with pros/cons (10–16)
- Multiple alternatives with full analysis (17–20)

### 3. Solution & Implementation (0–20)
- No solution (0)
- Solution without plan (1–9)
- Logical solution & plan (10–16)
- Feasible solution with resources (17–20)

### 4. Knowledge Areas Demonstrated (0–20)
- None (0)
- One–two (1–9)
- Three (10–16)
- Four or more (17–20)

### 5. Organization (0–10)
- Unprepared (0)
- Poor flow (1–6)
- Logical flow (7–8)
- Well-organized (9–10)

### 6. Delivery Skills (0–10)
- None (0)
- 1–2 skills (1–6)
- 3 skills (7–8)
- All skills (9–10)

### 7. Question Handling (0–10)
- Unable to answer (0)
- Partial answers (1–6)
- Complete answers (7–8)
- Judge interaction (9–10)
