**COMPETITOR INSTRUCTIONS**

1.  This is an interactive, role-playing event.

2.  You have **20 minutes** to review the role play scenario and prepare
    for the role play.

3.  Presentation time is **seven minutes**. Competitors will be given a
    one-minute warning indicated by the timekeeper standing and holding
    up a colored card. At seven minutes the timekeeper will stand and
    hold up a colored card indicating time is up. Competitors must stop
    when time is up.

4.  The presentation is an interactive role play with the judges who may
    ask questions during the seven minutes.

5.  Each competitor will be given two note cards to make notes. Note
    cards can be used during the role play. Note cards will be collected
    following the role play.

6.  Cover all the points described in the role play.

7.  If competing as a team, all competitors must participate in the role
    play as well as answer the questions posed by the judges.

**OBJECTIVES OF ROLE PLAY**

You must address the following during the role play:

- Describe the different living and working conditions found in various
  countries.

- Assess how social and cultural factors influence the human resource
  functions (e.g., recruitment selection; employee development;
  evaluation; compensation, promotion, benefit, and incentives; and
  separation, termination, and transition).

- Identify how motivational techniques for workers may differ when used
  in different cultures.

- Assess the impact of various occupational health and safety standards
  on conducting international business.

- Explain the advantages and disadvantages of a company having access to
  a global labor pool.
