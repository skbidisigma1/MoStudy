**COMPETITOR INSTRUCTIONS**

1.  This is an interactive, role-playing event.

2.  You have **20 minutes** to review the role play scenario and prepare
    for the role play.

3.  Presentation time is **seven minutes**. Competitors will be given a
    one-minute warning indicated by the timekeeper standing and holding
    up a colored card. At seven minutes the timekeeper will stand and
    hold up a colored card indicating time is up. Competitors must stop
    when time is up.

4.  The presentation is an interactive role play with the judges who may
    ask questions during the seven minutes. If there are multiple
    judges, they may designate one judge to lead the interaction while
    the others observe.

5.  Each competitor will be given two note cards to make notes. Note
    cards can be used during the role play. Note cards will be collected
    following the role play.

6.  Cover all the points described in the role play.

7.  If competing as a team, all competitors must participate in the role
    play as well as answer the questions posed by the judges.

**ROLE PLAY SCENARIO**

<td><p><strong>Background</strong></p>
<p>The Beanery, an American multinational coffeehouse chain, has long
been a leader in business. It is looking into expanding internationally
to other markets. One market of interest is India. India, a rapidly
developing nation with a rich cultural heritage, presents both
opportunities and challenges for the company. India has a growing GDP,
improving infrastructure, and expanding middle class but still faces
challenges like income disparity and literacy issues.</p>
<p><strong>Scenario</strong></p>
<p>You are part of the research team for global expansion. You are
preparing some criteria for developing priorities for the expansion.
Prepare the following information.</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Expanded Markets: Develop four criteria to utilize for choosing
expansion markets in the new country.</p></li>
<li><p>Pricing: Describe a pricing strategy for the new market. How will
you approach pricing for communities of varying economic backgrounds and
in relation to the cost of living in the markets.</p></li>
<li><p>Branding: Create two branding ideas for the new venture to be
utilized in the expansion.</p></li>
</ul></td>

**OBJECTIVES OF ROLE PLAY**

You must address the following during the role play:

- Describe the decision-making process, opportunity costs, and scarcity
  as they relate to international business situations.

- Discuss the effect of literacy level, technology, natural resource
  availability, and infrastructure on the level of a country's economic
  development.

- Identify risks and rewards related to doing business in a foreign
  market.

- List factors that affect international business competition.

- Describe what a company must consider when marketing a product/service
  in other countries.

- Illustrate how social, cultural, technological, and geographic factors
  influence consumer buying behavior in different cultures.

- Describe how language, culture, media availability, and regulations
  affect international advertising and marketing.
